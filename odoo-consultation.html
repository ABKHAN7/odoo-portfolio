Common questions

Things buyers ask before booking.

Do you work on both Odoo Community and Enterprise?+

Yes — both editions, versions 14 through 19. Most of my recent work is on 17, 18, and 19. I'll tell you upfront if a feature requires Enterprise so you can factor licensing into the decision.

Will you fix a previous developer's mess?+

Often, yes — that's actually a frequent engagement. Start with the Diagnostic Sprint so I can audit what's there, identify what's salvageable, and quote the cleanup honestly. I won't pretend nothing's wrong to win the project.

How do you handle scope creep?+

Every engagement starts with a written scope document. Anything outside that scope gets a written change order with its own estimate and timeline. You stay in control — no surprise invoices.

Can you sign an NDA?+

Yes. I regularly sign mutual NDAs before discovery calls when client data or proprietary processes are involved. Send yours, or use mine.

Do you host or just develop?+

Both options available. I deploy to Odoo.sh, your own VPS (Nginx + SSL hardened), or hybrid setups. Hosting choice usually depends on data residency, budget, and team familiarity — happy to advise.

What happens after the engagement ends?+

You own everything — source code, documentation, deployment configs. A 14-day hypercare window is included on every Build & Ship engagement. After that you can extend on a retainer or hire your in-house team to maintain it.
